# 118. MACRO INTELLIGENCE LAYER OVERVIEW

The Macro Intelligence Layer is the fourth major intelligence layer of the project.

Purpose:

To measure the economic environment in which markets operate.

The OI Layer answers:

"What are derivatives traders doing?"

The FII/DII Layer answers:

"What are institutions doing?"

The News Layer answers:

"What information entered the market?"

The Macro Layer answers:

"What economic forces are driving the market?"

This distinction is extremely important.

Many market moves occur not because of technical signals but because the entire economic environment changes.

---

Markets do not operate in isolation.

Every market exists within a larger economic system.

Therefore:

Ignoring macro conditions creates an incomplete model.

---

# 120. CORE HYPOTHESIS

The project assumes:

Price behavior is regime dependent.

The same OI signal may produce:

Bullish outcomes

in one macro regime

and

Bearish outcomes

in another.

Therefore:

Macro information acts as a regime filter.

---

# 121. MACRO LAYER OBJECTIVE

The objective is not:

Predict macroeconomics.

The objective is:

Quantify the current economic environment.

and

Transform macro conditions into machine-readable features.
