# 254. FUSION LAYER OVERVIEW

The Fusion Layer is the central intelligence coordination engine of the entire project.

Every intelligence layer built before this point exists for one purpose:

To provide structured information to the Fusion Layer.

The Fusion Layer is where:

OI Intelligence

FII/DII Intelligence

News Intelligence

Macro Intelligence

Mathematical Intelligence

Market Data Intelligence

all converge into a unified decision-making framework.

---

# 255. WHY A FUSION LAYER EXISTS

Most retail systems make a fundamental mistake.

They attempt:

Raw Features
→
ML Model
→
Trade

This architecture is extremely fragile.

Reason:

Different information sources often disagree.

Example:

News Layer:

Bullish

OI Layer:

Bearish

FII/DII Layer:

Bullish

Macro Layer:

Bearish

Volatility Layer:

Dangerous

What should the system do?

Without a Fusion Layer:

Nobody knows.
