# FII/DII Intelligence Features (Version 1)

This document describes the exact ML-ready features generated by the FII/DII Intelligence Layer analytics engine. 

## 1. Raw Flow Features
- `fii_buy`, `fii_sell`, `fii_net`
- `dii_buy`, `dii_sell`, `dii_net`
- **Purpose:** Baseline tracking of foreign vs. domestic cash deployments. 

## 2. Divergence Features
- `fii_dii_divergence` = `fii_net` - `dii_net`
- **Purpose:** Measures capital conflict. A high positive number indicates FIIs are injecting massive capital while DIIs are relatively quiet or selling. Extreme divergences often precede major structural trend shifts in the underlying index.

## 3. Rolling Flow Features
- `fii_3d_flow`, `fii_5d_flow`, `fii_10d_flow`, `fii_20d_flow`
- `dii_3d_flow`, `dii_5d_flow`, `dii_10d_flow`, `dii_20d_flow`
- **Purpose:** Institutional repositioning does not happen in a single day. Rolling windows act as low-pass filters, smoothing out single-day volatility and detecting sustained Accumulation or Distribution cycles. 

## 4. Participation Features
- `total_institutional_flow` = `abs(fii_net) + abs(dii_net)`
- `fii_flow_share` = `abs(fii_net) / total_institutional_flow`
- `dii_flow_share` = `abs(dii_net) / total_institutional_flow`
- **Purpose:** Detects who is currently dominating the market structure. If FII flow share drops below 10%, the market is entirely driven by domestic retail and mutual funds.

## 5. Streak Features
- `fii_buy_streak`, `fii_sell_streak`
- `dii_buy_streak`, `dii_sell_streak`
- **Purpose:** Mathematically captures momentum. If FII sells for 5 consecutive days (`fii_sell_streak = 5`), the market is under immense supply pressure.
